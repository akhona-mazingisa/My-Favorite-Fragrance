Goldea by Bvlgari is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. Goldea was launched in 2015. The nose behind this fragrance is Alberto Morillas. Top notes are Musk, Raspberry, Orange Blossom and Bergamot; middle notes are Ylang-Ylang, Musk and Jasmine; base notes are Amber, Musk, Patchouli and Papyrus.
